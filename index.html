Our Objectives

UNITING YOUTH

We are a completey youth led initiative and believe that change in society can only be brought by the youth.

ORGANISING CAMPS

Organizing blood donation camps is one of our core activities. All our camps focus on giving blood donor the best experience. Learn more on how to organize a camp.

RUNNING HELPLINE

We run a 24x7, 365 days helpline to help those who need blood urgently. If you need support, raise a blood request here, we will try our level best to help you!

SAVING LIVES

We work with a sole objective of saving lives. We act as a channel connecting voluntary donors with those who need blood. Till now, we have helped save over 20,000 lives!
